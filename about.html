About Our Hospital

Vedant Group of Hospitals is one of the fastest growing chain providing world class healthcare services. Vedant Central is a state of the art multi superspecialty hospital providing a complete spectrum of advanced medical and surgical care at very affordable prices. The hospital systems are based on cutting edge technologies to assure latest health care services to patients of all age groups. We proudly underline our commitment to reaching the benefits of high quality healthcare services to the weaker sections of society.

Our Expertise

Certified Doctors

From modern facilities to highly qualified certified doctors. There are a number of Super-speciality hospitals in town but finding a perfect combination of top certified doctors and best modern facilities might be difficult. From well equipped advanced machinery to the best in town experts and doctors, here is Vedant Central Hospital that is giving patients a new ray of hope in life..

Personalised Patient Care

Personalized patient care is what sets Vedant Central Hospital apart. When you visit our hospital you can expect to receive world class care. Expert physician specialists and caring clinical staff provide you with an exceptional health care experience.

Modern Medical Facilities

We have state of Art facilities and most modern equipment to provide medical care to our patients. Vedant Central Hospital is well equipped with most accurate monitoring systems, ventilators, advanced machinery to perform surgeries..

24/7 Medical Call Centre

We have a dedicated 24/7 medical call centre to provide excellent service and care to our patients.
